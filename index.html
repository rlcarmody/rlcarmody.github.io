With 10 years of user-facing support, I revel in uncovering user’s pain points and finding creative solutions to suit their needs. As a habitual tinkerer and problem solver, I anguished over the times that the problem was caused by design and I was powerless to correct it. My desire to transition from tinkerer to builder drove me to attend the University of Oregon Coding Boot Camp to finally satisfy my thirst for empowerment to engineer real solutions rather than just workarounds when a user’s need is not met. I am always looking to broaden my understanding of different topics.

I am comfortable with front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, and jQuery as well as SQL queries. I also have experience using service and collaboration platforms like Sharepoint, Confluence, JIRA and Team Foundation Server and I am a Certified Salesforce Administrator.
